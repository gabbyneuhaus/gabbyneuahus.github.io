About Us

Welcome to Java Been - where Atmosphere, Company, and Coffee converge to create an unparalleled experience. Steeped in a history that spans just under three years, Java Been has transformed from a mere idea to a vibrant, bustling cafe that pulses with life and creativity. Our origins are rooted in the heart of a community seeking a haven for inspiration, collaboration, and connection. Nestled within the walls of a space with high ceilings and an enchanting "found space" allure, Java Been has become more than just a cafe; it's an integral hub for exhibitions, meetings, and local musical talents. Our story intertwines with the narrative of an arts revival community that has breathed new life into the old factory mill buildings along the tranquil riverbanks.

Our vision is a beacon that guides us forward. We take immense pride in nurturing our community, crafting an environment that fuels the flames of creativity.
